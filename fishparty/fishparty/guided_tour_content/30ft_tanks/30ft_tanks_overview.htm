30ft Tanks

Catchable sized rainbow trout, Arctic grayling and Chinook salmon are reared in large 30’ diameter by 8’ deep tanks. These tanks, holding about 40,00 gallons each, are operated as 95-97% recirculation systems. In order to operate at this high level of recirculation, significant filtration is required. This includes a radial flow separator, drum filter, pump sump, biofilter, bypass column, gas conditioning tower and low head oxygenator. Fish are typically reared in these tanks for 9-10 months before being stocked out into area lakes as 7-10” catchable sized fish
