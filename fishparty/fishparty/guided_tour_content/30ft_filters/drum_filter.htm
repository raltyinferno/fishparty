30ft Tank Filters

Drum Filter

Water from the radial flow separator and sidebox drains are combined before entering the drum filter. The drum filter consists of a large fiberglass enclosure which houses a screened drum. Water enters into the center of the drum, passing through the 89 micron screen and out of the housing. Solids are trapped on the inside of the screen and removed from the system by an automatic backwashing system. The solids are discharged to the domestic sewer system.
